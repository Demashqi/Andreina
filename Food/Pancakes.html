Morning Mini Pancakes


Dusted with Shamrock golden sugar, a squeez of lemon and a drizzle of honey on top.. I believe there's nothing better in the morning for breakfast!
Instructions
Sieve the flour into a bowl and add your salt and eggs. Mix both until it thicken goes rough.
Add butter and half the milk. Whisk through and keep adding milk until you see no more lumps.
Once you've added enough milk and you have a consistent smooth recipe, add sugar and a tespoon of cinnamon and mix.
Heat your pan to medium and add in the batter.
Flip the pancake to both sides until golden cooked and you are ready to add in your toppings!
Enjoy!!!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 300g flour
  • 2 spoons of sugar (extra for pancake topping)
  • 1 spoon melted butter
  • Pinch of salt
  • 600ml milk
  • Cinnamon
  • Honey (for topping)
