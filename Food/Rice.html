Garlic Butter Rice


The Garlic Butter Rice is the perfect side for any meal. It's full of flavour; buttery, garlicky - and so good you can eat it plain!
Instructions
Heat Olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic slices and saute, moving constantly, until golden and crisp.
Add your rice and butter of preferrence and stir for 2 mins.
Next add broth, place lid on saucepan and bring to simmer. Then immediately turn down to medium low.
Cook for 12-15 minutes or until liquid is all absorbed bt the rice. Tilt saucepan to check.
Remove from stove but leave lid on, and let it rest for 10 mins.
Fluff the rice with a fork, ad transfer into serving bowl. Stir through remaining butter and scallions, or garnish as per photos.
Sprikle with crispy garlic, salt, peper and you're ready to eat!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 4-6 cloves garlic
  • 2-3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • Scallions
  • Salt
  • Pepper
