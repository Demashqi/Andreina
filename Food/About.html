A Little About Moi


With Passion For Real, Good Food

Hey there, my name is Andreina Carvalho (but you can call me Driina). I have a heart of love and interest in cuisine. I want to share my world with you. Share all the little bits I've learned in the kitchen while trying out new crazy ideas. Many Grand Chefs/Bakers started by adding different spices, others by combining different ingredients so why not join them in this journey. The Kitchen is a world where you transform, imagine, renovate, and re-invent the true meaning of "FOOD". On this web page, I intend to share my favorite recipes (both Sweet & Salty), just some simple but flavored goodies. I hope you enjoy it!
